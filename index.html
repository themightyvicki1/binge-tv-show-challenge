  • About

    Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D is a superhero action packed spin-off series based on the character, Phil Coulson, played by Clark Gregg. Coulson is first introduced in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) movie, Iron Man (2008). The character is seen throughout five movies of the phase one of the MCU. He is believed to have been killed off in The Avengers (2012). His death is used as a driving force to bring together the other super-heroes so they can defeat their common enemy. The series follows the character, who is brought back from the dead, and answers questions of how he is brought back and why, while he builds his own secret team to protect the world.
    Themes seen in the series include time travel, enhanced individuals, doing what is right, and finding family in an unlikely set of individuals. There 136 episodes of the series spread out through seven seasons. The run-time is roughly 45 minutes.
